HypeTrain is the spare time project of Ursa Major Games, a group of students at UCSC and elsewhere. I wear many hats in the sporadic creation of this run-and-gun action game about robbing a very, very long train. As Art Lead, I direct the game's visual style, provide feedback to other artists, and create character, environment, and object assets. As Designer, I contributed to the game's core mechanics, as well as designing traincars and NPCs such as the Robo Falconeer boss and Tucker the Buddy AI Dog. As a programmer I have implemented aforementioned characters and assets using C# and the Unity engine, working within a codebase that we have built from the ground up.
