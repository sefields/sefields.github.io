UCSC Meal Builder was created for UCSC's Fall 2015 Software Engineering course, CMPS 115. Our app is targeted at the average hungry student and contains locations, hours, and menus of on-campus eateries, as well as functionality for those who really love to micro-manage their meal plans and squeeze out every last cent.
