Stealth Invasion! Crimea was created for UCSC's Winter 2015 Game Programming course, CMPM 20. Students were tasked with creating a game based on current world events. We satirized Russia's recent "stealth invasion" of Ukraine with an arcade style game about hiding one soldier at a time, in obvious places and to humorous effect.
