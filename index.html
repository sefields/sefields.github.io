FeatherWeight

Previous Next

FeatherWeight is a Twitch-integrated arena action game. Twitch chatters vote on what challenges and creatures the streamer will face in each round, with the streamer's ultimate goals being survival and collection of as many chickens as possible. I collaboratively designed and iterated upon core mechanics and programmed movement, NPC behavior, and combat, contributing to other systems whenever necessary.

  • Role: Lead Developer
  • Technologies Unity, C#
  • Team: Team Nomad (12 members)
  • Download: http://whos-watching.me

UCSC Meal Builder

Previous Next

UCSC Meal Builder is targeted at the average hungry student and contains locations, hours, and menus of on-campus eateries, as well as functionality for those who really love to micro-manage their meal plans and squeeze out every last cent. Created for Software Engineering course.

  • Role: Front End Developer, SCRUM Master
  • Technologies: Java, Android Studio, mySQL Lite
  • Team: "Team CHAMP" (5 members)

Stealth Invasion! Crimea

Stealth Invasion! Crimea satirizes Russia's recent "stealth invasion" of Ukraine with an arcade style game about hiding one soldier at a time, in obvious places and to humorous effect. Created for Game Programming course.

What Do We Do Meow?

Previous Next

What Do We Do Meow? was created in 48 hours during Global Game Jam 2015, the theme of which was "What do we do now?". Our game explored this question in the context of a happy couple who suddenly live in different places. I co-designed the game and narrative with my teammates, and created all art assets.

  • Role: Designer, Artist
  • Technologies: Unity, Adobe Photoshop
  • Team: Team What Do We Do Meow? (5 members)

HypeTrain

Previous Next

HypeTrain is a run-and-gun action game about robbing a very, very long train. It has been a spare-time project of myself and several other students. Although I started off on this project as Art Lead, I have worn many hats and have done such tasks as creating a buddy AI and overhauling the "Hype" power-up system using a more object-oriented design.

  • Role: Programmer, Artist, Designer
  • Technologies: Unity, C#, Adobe Photoshop
  • Team: Ursa Major Games
