Panopticon is being created for UCSC's 2016 Senior Design series. It is a survival game that is built for streaming over Twitch.tv, with viewers affecting change in the game world to thwart the streamer. We conceived of Panopticon as a title that would fully leverage the unique streamer-viewer interaction that Twitch provides, and we are excited to be exploring such a challenging and innovative design space. Panopticon is slated to release in June 2016.
