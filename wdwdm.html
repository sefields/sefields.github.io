What Do We Do Meow? was created in 48 hours during Global Game Jam 2015 at UCSC. "What do we do now?" was the theme of the jam. Our game explored this question in the context of a happy couple who suddenly live in different places. I was happy to contribute design of mini-games and narrative, as well as creation of all art assets.
